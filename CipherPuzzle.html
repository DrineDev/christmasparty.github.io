Solve the problem with a cipher.

The wind howled outside as Santa sat in his quiet study, holding an ancient scroll sealed with a golden laurel stamp. The parchment bore only a single line of cryptic text:

Btggn Rwgxhibph!

There was no salutation, no sender. Only a brief note scribbled at the bottom:

“To unlock the gift of time, follow the footsteps of a leader by the river.”

Santa furrowed his brow. “Time… a leader… the river.” He reached for a dusty book labeled Forgotten Codes of Antiquity. On its first page, the name of a Roman general stood out boldly. "Ah, Julius... it seems you’ve left another challenge.”

He studied the scroll again. “Fifteen winters past the first day... perhaps that’s the answer,” he mused. Pulling out his trusty cipher wheel, Santa aligned the letters and began to decode.
